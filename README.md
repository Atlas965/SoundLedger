# SoundLedger

SoundLedger is an independent electronic music platform designed to showcase artists, releases, and music videos in a modern web experience. The platform serves as a centralized hub for electronic music discovery, artist promotion, and digital distribution visibility.

SoundLedger focuses on highlighting electronic music while providing a clean and scalable artist-page system similar to platforms like GRM Daily and 6ixBuzz.

---

## Overview

SoundLedger is a web platform that allows:

• Promotion of electronic music artists
• Publishing of music releases and cover art
• Showcasing music videos
• Direct linking to digital streaming platforms
• Artist discovery and music browsing

The platform acts as a digital music publication and promotional hub.

---

## Features

* Artist profile pages

## Distribution & Streaming

Music promoted on SoundLedger may be distributed through digital distribution platforms such as UnitedMasters.

The platform may link to streaming services including:

* Spotify
* Apple Music
* YouTube Music

SoundLedger primarily functions as a promotional and discovery platform rather than a primary DSP.

---

## Vision

The long-term vision for SoundLedger is to develop a scalable digital media platform for electronic music culture, similar to leading music discovery platforms but focused on electronic music genres.

Future development goals include:

* Artist upload dashboard
* Global music player
* Music analytics dashboard
* Community discovery tools
* Mobile-optimized experience
